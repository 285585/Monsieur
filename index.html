About Me

Bonjour! My name is Mohammed, and I’m passionate about the French language and culture. I’ve dedicated my career to helping students of all ages and backgrounds discover the beauty and richness of French. With years of experience in teaching, I have developed a deep understanding of the unique challenges and rewards that come with learning a new language.

I believe that language learning is not just about grammar and vocabulary but also about immersing oneself in the culture and history that shape the language. In my classes, I strive to create an engaging and supportive environment where students feel encouraged to express themselves and take risks in their language learning journey.
